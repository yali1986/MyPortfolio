About me

As part of my work experience, I worked for two years as a journalist at a national newspaper with a digital edition, and I also collaborated with other media outlets. I published articles and photographs.

Currently, I have been working for over four years as an administrative assistant at the same company, while spending the past two years fully dedicated to intensive full-time studies in programming and development. I have obtained a level-three professional certification, and I graduated from the in-person IT Academy bootcamp with a specialization in React, as well as the Frontend bootcamp with a focus on Vue from Code Op school.

During this intense training period, I developed strong time management skills, the ability to deliver work in sprints, solve problems independently, and collaborate effectively in team environments. As a developer, I worked in a web design and development cooperative using Agile methodologies. Currently, I’m developing applications that can be useful in both my personal life and at my current workplace. I’m now seeking to transition into the tech industry and join a development team as a Front-end developer.

In addition to programming, I enjoy writing, editing, nature, animals, and photography. I’m someone who loves learning every day. I live in Barcelona.
